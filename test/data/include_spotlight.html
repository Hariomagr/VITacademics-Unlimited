CIRCULAR

 

Sub: VITAA Book Bank Scheme - Donate Your Used Books

-:-

 

VITAA (VIT Alumni Association) requests the students to donate the used books to the VITAA Book Bank.  The VITAA Book Bank scheme helps the needy students with used books for a semester.  The needy students (STARS, GV School Development Programme, etc.,) can borrow the books from the VITAA Book Bank in Periyar EVR Central Library.


The students who are willing to donate the books are requested either to drop them in the collection boxes placed at the entrance of all the hostel blocks or to hand over the books at the VITAA Office, Room no. 209, 2nd floor, Dr. M.G.R. Block.
